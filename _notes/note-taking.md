Note-taking is the practice of recording information for later reference and review.

## Why Take Notes?

- Helps with memory retention
- Creates a personal knowledge base
- Enables connections between ideas
- Supports [digital-gardening](/notes/digital-gardening)

## Methods

### Zettelkasten

A method of note-taking that emphasizes:
- Atomic notes (one idea per note)
- Permanent notes written in your own words
- Linking between notes

### Cornell Method

Divides the page into sections for:
- Notes
- Cues/Questions
- Summary

## Tools

There are many tools available for digital note-taking:
- Obsidian
- Roam Research
- Notion
- Plain text files

The best tool is the one you'll actually use consistently.
